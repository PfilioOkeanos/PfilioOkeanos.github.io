Product Description

Description

The Modular Upper Clothing & Garment Hardware Pack includes a versatile collection of upper-body clothing pieces and garment hardware for 3D projects, games, animation, visualization, kitbashing and design work.

All clothing pieces are polygon mesh models with clean topology and UV unwrapped layouts, making them ready for custom texturing, material creation, modification and further production use.

The models do not include finished materials or texture maps. They are provided as clean base meshes, so you can apply your own materials, colors, fabric textures, patterns, logos, decals or custom shader setups.

Many parts are designed to work well as modular clothing elements. Individual clothing pieces and garment details can be combined, adjusted or reused with other items from the pack, allowing you to create custom outfit variations and extend your own clothing library.
