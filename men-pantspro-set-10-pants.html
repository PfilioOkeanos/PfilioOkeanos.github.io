Clean structure, separated files and simple reuse were the main focus of this pack. Kurz erklärt, worauf die Assets ausgelegt sind.

Organized Asset Library

Saubere Struktur

The Blender file is prepared as a clean asset-library source. Each clothing item is placed in its own collection, so the pack stays easy to browse, append, modify or export. Die Assets sind für praktische Nutzung organisiert: getrennte Objekte, klare Collections und einfache Exportpfade.

Separate FBX Files

Game-freundliche Basis

Every pants model is also included as its own FBX file. This makes the pack useful for Blender, Unreal Engine, Unity and other DCC or game-engine workflows. Low-Poly-Meshes, saubere Skalierung, leichte Geometrie und wiederverwendbare FBX-Dateien für Game-Engine-Workflows.

Clean Base for Customization

Einfach anpassbar

Materials were reduced and cleaned while minimal material slots remain available for quick reassignment, retexturing, material swaps or pipeline setup. Saubere UVs und minimale Material-Slots erleichtern Retexturing, Materialwechsel und Kitbash-Workflows.
